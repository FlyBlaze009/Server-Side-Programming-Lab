About Union Station

Union Station is the largest railroad passenger terminal in the western United States, showcasing a blend of Spanish Colonial, Mission Revival, and Art Deco architectural styles.

History

Since its opening in 1939, Union Station has been a vital transportation hub and an iconic symbol of Los Angeles' rich architectural heritage.
