About Hollywood Bowl

The Hollywood Bowl is an iconic amphitheater famous for its unique shell-shaped design and world-class musical performances that draw audiences from around the globe.

History

Since its opening in 1922, the Hollywood Bowl has become synonymous with Los Angeles' entertainment scene and continues to host unforgettable live events.
