About The Historic Theatre

The Historic Theatre in Los Angeles is a landmark built in 1920. Known for its unique architecture and rich cultural history, the theatre has hosted countless performances and events over the decades.

History

Originally constructed as a vaudeville house, The Historic Theatre has undergone several restorations. Its architecture reflects the grand style of early 20th-century design, and today it serves as a reminder of Los Angeles' vibrant entertainment past.
