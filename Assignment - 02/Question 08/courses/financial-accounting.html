About This Course

This course introduces students to financial accounting principles, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow management. Students will learn how to analyze financial data for business decision-making.

Course Instructors

  • Dr. Warren Buffett - Professor of Finance
  • Prof. Benjamin Graham - Investment Strategies Specialist
  • Dr. Mary T. Barth - Financial Reporting Expert
