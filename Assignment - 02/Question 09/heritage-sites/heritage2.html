About The Getty Center

The Getty Center is renowned for its stunning architecture and breathtaking views of Los Angeles. It houses an impressive collection of European paintings, decorative arts, and sculptures.

History

Opened in 1997, The Getty Center has quickly become one of Los Angeles' premier cultural landmarks, combining modern design with classical art collections.
