About The Broad Museum

The Broad is a contemporary art museum that features an extensive collection of postwar and contemporary art, attracting art enthusiasts from around the world.

History

Opened in 2015, The Broad has rapidly become a cultural landmark, showcasing the dynamic art scene of Los Angeles.
