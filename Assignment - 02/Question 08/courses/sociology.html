About This Course

The Sociology course examines human society, culture, and social institutions. Students will analyze social behavior, interactions, and the impact of social change.

Course Instructors

  • Dr. Max Weber - Professor of Social Theory
  • Prof. Emile Durkheim - Sociological Research Specialist
  • Dr. Jane Addams - Community Studies Expert
