About This Course

The Business Economics course covers microeconomics, macroeconomics, and market analysis. Students will develop analytical skills to evaluate economic policies and business strategies.

Course Instructors

  • Dr. Adam Smith - Professor of Microeconomics
  • Prof. John Maynard Keynes - Macroeconomics Specialist
  • Dr. Paul Krugman - Economic Policy Expert
