About El Pueblo de Los Angeles

El Pueblo de Los Angeles marks the birthplace of the city. This historic district is filled with vibrant cultural landmarks and traditions that celebrate the city’s diverse roots.

History

Founded in the late 18th century, El Pueblo has preserved the early history of Los Angeles, offering visitors a glimpse into the city’s multicultural past.
