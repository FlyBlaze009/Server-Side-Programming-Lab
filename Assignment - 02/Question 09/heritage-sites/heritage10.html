About Bradbury Building

The Bradbury Building is famous for its extraordinary interior, featuring intricate ironwork and a stunning glass atrium that has captivated architects and film makers alike.

History

Constructed in 1893, this historic building is one of Los Angeles' oldest architectural treasures and continues to inspire with its timeless design.
