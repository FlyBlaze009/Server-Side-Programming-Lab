About This Course

The English course at Princeton University explores literature, language, and writing from historical and contemporary perspectives. Students will engage in critical analysis, creative writing, and discussions on major literary works, authors, and theories. The course covers poetry, drama, fiction, and non-fiction while enhancing students' communication and analytical skills.

Course Instructors

  • Dr. Emily Carter - Professor of Literature
  • Prof. Jonathan Smith - Creative Writing Specialist
  • Dr. Rachel Adams - Linguistics and Language Studies
