About This Course

The Computer Science course at Princeton University covers programming, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems. Students will gain hands-on experience in coding, problem-solving, and software development.

Course Instructors

  • Dr. Alan Turing - Professor of Algorithms
  • Prof. Grace Hopper - Software Engineering Specialist
  • Dr. Ada Lovelace - Computational Theory Expert
